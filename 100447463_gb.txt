George Brown College

George Brown College is located in Toronto, Ontario. It was established in 1967
and was named after George Brown who was one of the Fathers of Confederation. The college
offers over 160 career-focused programs, including diplomas, degrees and certificates.
There are 3 main campuses which include:
Casa Loma Campus (Technology and Skilled Trades)
St. James Campus (Business and Community Services)
Waterfront Campus (Health Sciences)

I currently attend the Casa Loma Campus which focuses on technology, construction,
engineering and computer related programs. It provides modern labs, computer classrooms
and collaborative learning spaces.

There are many career opportunities for Computer System Technician students after graduation, they include:
IT Support Specialist
Network Administrator
Systems Technician
Help Desk Analyst
Junior DevOps Support Technician

The T-191 Program prepares students with practical technical skills that are required in the IT industry.

In conclusion, George Brown College provides strong practical and technical education. The Computer Systems
Technician program equips students with hands-on skills that prepares them for careers in IT and DevOps related
roles.
